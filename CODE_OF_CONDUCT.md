# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful space for everyone.

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions.
- Assume good intent in discussions and code reviews.
- Share feedback about code, not people.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get oriented.
- Use inclusive language in all communications.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or inappropriate comments.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to `security@stacksclicker.io`.

### What to Include

- Description of the incident
- Dates and relevant context
- Any supporting evidence (screenshots, logs, etc.)
- Whether the incident is ongoing

All reports are handled in confidence. We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of anyone who reports an incident.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Those who do not follow the code of conduct may face temporary or permanent consequences, including removal from the project.
